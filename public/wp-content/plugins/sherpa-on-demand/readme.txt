== Description ==
Connects your WooCommerce store to your Sherpa Delivery account. Automated same day (and future day) local delivery for Australian businesses.
Let Sherpa Delivery help with your express same day shipping, local delivery and last-mile fulfilment, so you can focus on growing your business.

**Some of Our Amazing Features**
* Automatically send orders from your online store directly to Sherpa for delivery, or opt to ‘Send to Sherpa’ at another time
* Choose from multiple delivery options within Australian metro areas
* Automatically send orders from your online store directly to Sherpa for delivery
* Manage which products, delivery options and days you offer to your customers
* Control your store’s opening days and times
* Allow customers to choose a delivery date from a date picker
* Allow customer to select from multiple delivery windows, or on demand 
* Set a preparation time for your products. This time will be factored in when notifying a driver that the item(s) are ready for pickup
* Adjust delivery price based on distance from your store
* Specify delivery preferences and instructions for the driver
* Option to send tracking link to customer
* Option to test before putting live
* Set default product description for orders

**Driving Customer Success**

* Omni-channel driver and delivery support through live chat, phone and email
* Best-in-class customer support team trained to help you succeed
* Available 7 days a week, 7AM - 9PM (AEDT)

**How to Get Started**

* Sign up for a [Sherpa business account](<https://deliveries.sherpa.net.au/users/sign_up>)
* Install and activate WooCommerce on your site
* Install and activate Sherpa Delivery for WooCommerce plugin on your site
* Navigate to WooCommerce > Sherpa Delivery to add your Sherpa account login details (or QA test account login details) - select 'Test & Save'
* Enable the Sherpa Delivery shipping method by navigating to WooCommerce > Settings > Shipping > Sherpa and selecting 'Enable this shipping method'
* Enter your origin (pickup) address
* Scroll down to select which delivery services you will be offering to your customers
* Click 'Save changes' at the bottom of the page
* Return to WooCommerce > Sherpa Delivery to configure your delivery preferences and options
* Start delivering with Sherpa!

== Screenshots ==
1. Sherpa Account Details: Connect your WordPress WooCommerce site to your Sherpa account with the option to run test orders
2. Delivery Settings: Select which delivery services you make available to your customers, and what you call them
3. Delivery Rate & Distance: Set your pricing with the option to use Sherpa dynamically calculated rates, add a margin or specify flat rates depending on distance
4. Business Details: Manage the days and hours you offer delivery, how much time you need to prepare orders, and provide pickup instructions for the driver
5. Shipping Options at Checkout: Allow customers to select a delivery time now, a delivery window of their choice or a delivery date and time in the future

== Installation ==

= Minimum Requirements =

* WordPress 6.3.1 or greater is recommended
* PHP version 8.2 or greater is recommended
* MySQL version 8.0.16 or greater is recommended
* WooCommerce activated

= From the Wordpress Plugin Directory =
* In the left navigation, go to Plugins > Add New
* Search for 'Sherpa Delivery for WooCommerce'
* Click 'Install Now'
* Make sure WooCommerce plugin is activated
* Activate the plugin on the plugins dashboard
* Navigate to WooCommerce > Sherpa Delivery to add your Sherpa account login details (or QA test account login details) - select 'Test & Save'
* Enable the Sherpa Delivery shipping method by navigating to WooCommerce > Settings > Shipping > Sherpa and selecting 'Enable this shipping method'

= Manual Upload =
* Download the plugin from the [WordPress.org plugin directory](https://wordpress.org/plugins/sherpa-on-demand/)
* In the left navigation, go to Plugins > Add New
* Click on 'Upload'
* Select the Sherpa plugin zipped file on your computer
* Click 'Install Now'
* Make sure WooCommerce plugin is activated
* Activate the plugin in the plugin dashboard
* Navigate to WooCommerce > Sherpa Delivery to add your Sherpa account login details (or QA test account login details) - select 'Test & Save'
* Enable the Sherpa Delivery shipping method by navigating to WooCommerce > Settings > Shipping > Sherpa and selecting 'Enable this shipping method'
